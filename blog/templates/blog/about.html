Our Mission

The mission to create this website was to provide efficient content and services to users at minimum possible prices to students, aspirants and institutions. We provide quality content to our users. Tutors, Institutes, coachings or educators can also post free test series through our website for which we do not charge them. We charge only a mimimum revenue on purchases of tests that charge some amount from the purchaser.

Our Vision

We are making all effort to make it one stop solution for students preparing for any competitive exam or board exam. Our app will be released soon and we are working to add a nice feature for sharing videos, articles and PDFs as interaction is an effective tool for learning. Also, we are polishing our whole website to make it more user friendly. Please provide your suggestions and feedback, it will help us to deliver valuable products.
